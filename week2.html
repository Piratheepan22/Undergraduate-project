Detailed Update

This week I started working with a real hyperspectral dataset. I downloaded the Urban6 dataset, which is commonly used for testing hyperspectral unmixing techniques. I explored the structure of the dataset and understood how the data is organized across spatial and spectral dimensions. I also studied how to visualize the data by plotting spectral signatures. I was able to extract and plot the endmembers from the image and observe their unique spectral curves. This helped me understand how different materials reflect light differently across wavelengths, which is what makes HSI so powerful. Things I did This week:

  • Downloaded and explored the Urban6 hyperspectral dataset
  • Studied how the data is stored and how to load it properly
  • Plotted spectral signatures from selected pixels
  • Visualized endmembers and learned how their spectral shapes differ
